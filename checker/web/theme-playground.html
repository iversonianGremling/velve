velve · theme playground

Turn the seed dials on the left. Everything on the right — every surface, every text shade, the button, light & dark — is derived. Nothing here is a hand-picked colour except the two seeds.

Seed · you choose these

Two colours expressed as dials — hue (which colour), vividness (grey→neon), lightness is not a seed dial: roles pick it per tone. The greys are the same hue, barely tinted.

Derived roles · computed for you

A role = a colour's job. Each is the seed at a tone (a rung on its dark→light ladder). Polarity just flips which end of the ladder is the background.

The velve that means this

-- two seeds → the whole app
theme app
  seed
    neutral = oklch(0.5, <tint>, <hue>)
    accent  = oklch(0.5, <vivid>, <hue>)
  roles
    surface = neutral.tone(10)   -- page
    raised  = neutral.tone(11)   -- card
    text    = neutral.tone(1)    -- ink
    muted   = neutral.tone(4)
    primary = accent.tone(4)
    onPrimary = neutral.tone(11)

let dark = app with { polarity: Dark }

-- a card: only ROLES, no colours, no `dark:`
def digest(it):
  Surface raised radius=12 padding=20
    Text (it.title) role=text
    Text (it.blurb) role=text
    Row
      Text "3 min" role=muted
      Button "Read" primary
